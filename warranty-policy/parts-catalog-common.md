# Common Parts Catalog - Quick Reference

**Document Type:** Parts Reference
**Manufacturer:** Outer Rim Starship Manufacturing (ORSM)
**Last Updated:** 2847.09.14

## Purpose

This catalog lists the most commonly needed replacement parts for ORSM vessels. These are high-wear items that should be stocked aboard or are frequently ordered. For complete parts catalog, visit ORSM support portal or contact Parts & Service department.

## How to Order Parts

### Direct from ORSM
- **Subspace Portal:** orsm-parts.net
- **Subspace Frequency:** 122.5 MHz (Parts & Service)
- **At Port:** Visit ORSM service center (100+ locations in settled space)

**Information Needed:**
- Ship registration number
- Ship model/class
- Part number (from this catalog)
- Quantity needed
- Delivery location

**Lead Time:**
- **In stock:** 1-3 days (shipped to nearby station)
- **Special order:** 5-14 days (manufactured or transferred from warehouse)
- **Emergency:** Same day (at major stations, premium charge +50%)

### Third-Party Suppliers
- Many generic parts (filters, O-rings, bolts) available from ship supply stores
- Verify compatibility before purchasing
- ORSM parts guaranteed compatible; third-party may require adaptation

## Part Number Format

**ORSM part numbers follow this format:**

**[SYSTEM]-[COMPONENT]-[VARIANT]**

Example: **MAG-COIL-Q3-MK5**
- **MAG-COIL**: Magnetic coil (component)
- **Q**: Q-Core reactor (system)
- **3**: Size variant (1=small, 3=standard, 5=large)
- **MK5**: Mark V reactor (specific model)

**Generic suffix:**
- **-A**: Standard grade
- **-B**: Heavy duty grade
- **-R**: Rebuilt/refurbished
- **-E**: Emergency/expedited shipping

## Recommended Stock Levels by Ship Class

### Scout / Small Personal Vessel (1-2 crew)

**Critical (always stock):**
- Emergency supplies: O₂ bottles (4), thermal blankets (5), rations (14 days)
- Filters: HEPA (6), sediment (6), WRMS UV lamp (1)
- Hull: Emergency sealant kits (5)
- General: O-ring kit, fuse kit, flashlights (3)

**High Priority (stock if space/budget allows):**
- APU: O₂ sensor (1), CO₂ chemical (1 bag)
- Comms: VHF/UHF antenna (1 each)
- Engine: Injector O-rings (1 set), fuel filter (1)

### Freighter / Medium Vessel (4-8 crew)

**Critical:**
- All scout items × 2
- Engine: Acceleration coil (2), injector assembly (1)
- Life support: Electrolysis unit (1), RO membrane (1)
- Hull: Emergency sealant kits (10), patches (5 small, 2 large)

**High Priority:**
- Reactor: Coolant filter (2), temperature sensor (1)
- Comms: Antennas (1 each type), subspace crystal (1)
- Security: RFID cards (10), lock batteries (10)

### Large Vessel / Industrial (8+ crew, long journeys)

**Critical:**
- All freighter items × 2
- Complete spare parts inventory for all critical systems
- Professional service manual suite
- Expanded emergency supplies (60+ day food/water)

**High Priority:**
- Major components: Coolant pumps, water pumps, fans
- Full electrical and hardware kits
- Comprehensive sensor spares

## Warranty on Parts

**ORSM parts warranty:**
- **Standard:** 12 months or 10,000 operating hours
- **Extended:** 24 months (add 20% to part price)
- **Consumables:** No warranty (filters, chemicals, etc.)
- **Installation:** Must be installed per manual; improper installation voids warranty

**Returns:**
- Unused parts: 30-day return (10% restocking fee)
- Defective parts: Full refund/replacement (no restocking fee)
- Must have original packaging and documentation

## Emergency Parts Access

**Stuck without a critical part?**

1. **Check nearby ships**: Other vessels may have spare (barter/buy)
2. **Salvage**: Derelict ships (legal salvage, not theft)
3. **Fabricate**: If ship has 3D printer/fab shop, some parts can be fabricated
4. **Improvise**: Temporary repair until proper part obtained (see KB articles for guidance)
5. **Emergency delivery**: ORSM offers drone delivery at major stations (premium charge)
