## 1. Standard Warranty Coverage

### New Equipment Standard Warranty

**Q-Core Reactor Systems:**
- **Coverage Period:** 5 years or 10,000 operating hours, whichever comes first
- **What's Covered:** Manufacturing defects, component failures under normal operation
- **Parts:** Free replacement
- **Labor:** Covered at ORSM certified service centers
- **Models:** Mark III, Mark V, Mark VII

**Horizon-Class FTL Drives:**
- **Coverage Period:** 3 years or 1,000 FTL jumps, whichever comes first
- **What's Covered:** Manufacturing defects, drive component failures
- **Parts:** Free replacement
- **Labor:** Covered at ORSM certified service centers
- **Models:** Horizon-1, Horizon-2, Horizon-3

**Sublight Propulsion Systems:**
- **Coverage Period:** 5 years or 15,000 operating hours, whichever comes first
- **What's Covered:** Manufacturing defects, coil failures, control system defects
- **Parts:** Free replacement
- **Labor:** Covered at ORSM certified service centers
- **Models:** Model-3 Impeller, Model-5 Heavy Impeller

**Life Support Systems:**
- **Coverage Period:** 3 years from installation
- **What's Covered:** O₂ generators, CO₂ scrubbers, water recycling, atmospheric controls
- **Parts:** Free replacement
- **Labor:** Covered at ORSM certified service centers

**Communications Systems:**
- **Coverage Period:** 2 years from installation
- **What's Covered:** Transceivers, antennas, subspace emitters (crystal excluded)
- **Parts:** Free replacement
- **Labor:** Covered at ORSM certified service centers

**Hull and Structural Components:**
- **Coverage Period:** 10 years from manufacture (new ships only)
- **What's Covered:** Structural defects, weld failures, material defects
- **Exclusions:** Impact damage, micrometeorite damage, normal wear

**Sensors and Control Systems:**
- **Coverage Period:** 2 years from installation
- **What's Covered:** Sensor failures, control computer defects, software defects
- **Parts:** Free replacement
- **Labor:** Covered at ORSM certified service centers

### Replacement Parts Standard Warranty

**All ORSM Certified Parts:**
- **Coverage Period:** 1 year from purchase or installation date (whichever is later)
- **What's Covered:** Manufacturing defects, premature failure under normal use
- **Parts:** Free replacement
- **Labor:** Customer responsibility (unless replaced at ORSM service center)

**Rebuilt/Refurbished Parts:**
- **Coverage Period:** 90 days from purchase
- **What's Covered:** Same as new, but shorter period
- **Parts:** Free replacement
- **Labor:** Customer responsibility

**Purchase:** Contact ORSM customer service or visit service portal at parts.outerrimstarship.com

## 3. What's Covered

### Covered Situations:

**Manufacturing Defects:**
- Component fails due to defect in materials or workmanship
- System doesn't perform to published specifications
- Premature component failure under normal operating conditions

**Examples:**
- Reactor magnetic coil fails at 2,000 hours (normal lifespan: 10,000 hours)
- FTL drive won't reach rated distance due to manufacturing defect
- Life support O₂ generator fails after 6 months of normal use
- Weld failure in structural component with no impact damage

**Software Defects:**
- Control software crashes or malfunctions
- Firmware bugs causing system malfunction
- Software updates that cause problems

**Electrical/Electronic Failures:**
- Circuit board failures
- Sensor failures
- Control system failures
- Power supply failures

### What Happens Under Warranty:

1. **Diagnosis:** ORSM or certified technician diagnoses the issue
2. **Approval:** Warranty claim approved if covered
3. **Parts:** ORSM ships replacement parts at no charge
4. **Labor:**
   - At ORSM/certified center: Labor covered
   - At independent shop: Labor not covered (except Parts & Labor extended plan)
5. **Shipping:**
   - To ORSM: Customer pays shipping TO
   - From ORSM: ORSM pays shipping FROM
   - Expedited shipping: Customer pays difference if requested

## 4. What's NOT Covered (Exclusions)

### General Exclusions:

**Normal Wear and Tear:**
- Components at or beyond expected lifespan
- Gradual performance degradation over time
- Filters, consumables, routine maintenance items

**Examples of Normal Wear:**
- Reactor injectors after 12 months (require monthly cleaning)
- Fuel filters after 6 months
- FTL drive capacitors after 5 years (normal replacement item)
- Antenna corrosion from environmental exposure

**Improper Maintenance:**
- Failure caused by neglecting scheduled maintenance
- Use of incorrect or contaminated consumables
- Failure to follow maintenance procedures in manual

**Examples:**
- Reactor failure from contaminated fuel (see KB-167)
- Life support failure from not replacing filters per schedule
- System failure from running without required coolant replacement

**Misuse or Abuse:**
- Operating system beyond rated specifications
- Unauthorized modifications
- Improper installation
- Use for purposes not intended

**Examples:**
- Reactor run continuously at 110% rated power
- FTL drive used for distances beyond rated maximum
- Life support system used for twice the crew capacity
- Communication system tampered with or modified

**External Damage:**
- Collision damage
- Micrometeorite impacts
- Fire (external causes)
- Flooding/water damage
- Battle damage
- Lightning strikes

**Damage from Other Systems:**
- Power surge from reactor failure damages other systems
- Coolant leak damages surrounding components
- Fire spreads from one system to another

**Note:** First system failure may be covered, but consequential damage to other systems typically is not.

**Accidents and Acts of Nature:**
- Operator error
- Sabotage
- Theft
- Solar flares (unless system rated for protection)
- Asteroid impacts

**Unauthorized Repair or Modification:**
- Repairs by non-certified technicians (voids warranty)
- Use of non-ORSM parts (may void warranty)
- Modifications without ORSM approval
- Tampering with safety systems

**Important:** DIY repairs using ORSM procedures with ORSM parts generally OK, but document everything.

**Commercial Use Beyond Rating:**
- Using civilian-rated equipment for commercial heavy use
- Exceeding duty cycle ratings
- Operating in extreme environments without proper equipment

**Example:** Using a Scout-class reactor rated for light use in a heavy commercial freighter running 24/7.

**Consumables:**
- Fuel (D-T pellets)
- Coolant (after first fill)
- Filters (all types)
- Lubricants
- O-rings and gaskets (after initial installation)
- Batteries (after first 2 years)

**Software Issues from User Error:**
- Incorrect configuration by user
- User-caused data corruption
- Incompatible third-party software
- User-declined software updates that would have fixed issue

**Pre-Existing Conditions:**
- Problems that existed before warranty coverage began
- Issues disclosed at time of purchase and excluded
- Damage from previous ownership (used equipment)

**Failure to Register:**
- Warranty coverage requires product registration within 90 days of purchase
- Unregistered products: 90-day limited warranty only

**Cosmetic Issues:**
- Scratches, dents, discoloration that don't affect function
- Paint or finish wear
- Minor corrosion not affecting operation

## 5. Warranty Claims Process

### Step-by-Step Claim Process:

**Step 1: Contact ORSM Support**
- Call: ORSM Technical Support (24/7)
- Email: warranty@outerrimstarship.com
- Online: parts.outerrimstarship.com/warranty-claim

**Have Ready:**
- Product serial number
- Purchase date and location
- Description of problem
- Error codes (if any)
- Maintenance records
- Photos of issue (if applicable)

**Step 2: Initial Diagnosis**
- Support technician will guide you through diagnostics
- Goal: Confirm problem and identify failed component
- May require you to run tests or provide data
- Time: 15-60 minutes depending on complexity

**Step 3: Warranty Determination**
- Support determines if issue is covered under warranty
- You'll be told: Covered, Not Covered, or Needs Inspection
- If not covered, you'll be offered paid repair options

**Step 4: If Covered - Parts Shipment**
- ORSM ships replacement part(s)
- Shipped to you or to certified service center
- Tracking number provided
- Typical ship time: 2-5 days (depends on location)

**Step 5: Installation**
- **Option A:** DIY installation using provided instructions
- **Option B:** Install at ORSM/certified service center (labor covered)
- **Option C:** Install at independent shop (parts covered, you pay labor)

**Step 6: Return Failed Part**
- ORSM provides return shipping label
- Pack failed part safely
- Ship within 30 days
- Failure to return may result in charge for replacement part

**Step 7: Claim Closed**
- Once failed part received and confirmed, claim closed
- Replacement part has remaining warranty from original purchase
- OR 90 days, whichever is longer

### Warranty Claim Timeline:

- **Claim Submission:** 15-60 minutes (diagnosis)
- **Claim Decision:** Within 24 hours (usually same day)
- **Parts Shipment:** Within 1 business day of approval
- **Parts Arrival:** 2-5 days depending on location
- **Installation:** Depends on part and installation method
- **Total Time:** Typically 3-7 days from claim to resolution

### Expedited Warranty Service:

**Available for critical failures (ship stranded, life support, etc.):**
- Expedited parts shipping (overnight/2-day)
- Customer pays shipping cost difference
- Priority claim processing
- Remote technical support during installation

**To Request:** Specify "Emergency Warranty Claim" when contacting support

## 6. Warranty Limitations

### Limitation of Liability:

**ORSM's Total Liability Limited To:**
- Replacement of defective part/system, OR
- Refund of purchase price (at ORSM's option)

**ORSM NOT Liable For:**
- Consequential damages (lost income, cargo loss, etc.)
- Incidental damages (towing, lodging, alternate transportation)
- Property damage beyond the defective part itself
- Personal injury (except where prohibited by law from limiting)
- Punitive damages

**Example:**
If your reactor fails under warranty, ORSM will replace the reactor for free. ORSM is NOT liable for the 10,000 credits you lost while stranded, or towing fees, or the hotel cost while waiting for repair. Consider purchasing business interruption insurance separately.

**Exception:** Some jurisdictions don't allow limitation of consequential damages. If you're in such a jurisdiction, limitations may not apply to you.

### Implied Warranties:

**Merchantability and Fitness for Purpose:**
- ORSM products are warranted to be merchantable and fit for their intended purpose
- Duration: Same as express warranty period
- Some jurisdictions require longer implied warranties; local law applies

### Time Limit for Claims:

**You Must:**
- Notify ORSM of defect within 30 days of discovery
- File warranty claim within warranty period
- Return defective part within 30 days of receiving replacement

**Failure to meet timelines may result in claim denial.**

## 7. Registration Requirements

### Why Register:

- **Activates Full Warranty:** Unregistered products have 90-day warranty only
- **Proof of Purchase:** Creates record in case you lose receipt
- **Recall Notifications:** ORSM can contact you about safety recalls
- **Service History:** Centralized record of all service and claims

### How to Register:

**Online (Recommended):**
1. Visit: parts.outerrimstarship.com/register
2. Enter product serial number
3. Enter purchase date and location
4. Provide contact information
5. Submit

**By Comm:**
- Call ORSM Customer Service
- Provide product information
- Registration processed immediately

**By Mail:**
- Complete registration card (included with product)
- Mail to: ORSM Warranty Registration, [Address]
- Allow 2 weeks for processing

### Timeline:

**Must register within 90 days of purchase for full warranty.**

After 90 days: Limited warranty only (90 days from purchase)

### What to Register:

- Major systems (reactor, FTL drive, life support, etc.)
- High-value components (>500 credits)
- Safety-critical components
- No need to register: Consumables, minor parts (<100 credits)

## 8. Transferability

### Warranty Transfer Policy:

**Standard Warranty:**
- **Transferable:** Yes, once
- **Fee:** 100 credits transfer fee
- **Process:** New owner must contact ORSM with proof of sale/transfer
- **Coverage:** Remaining warranty period transfers to new owner
- **Limitations:** No additional transfers (one transfer maximum)

**Extended Warranty:**
- **Transferable:** Depends on plan
- **Premium Extended:** Not transferable
- **Comprehensive Extended:** Transferable once for 200 credit fee
- **Parts & Labor Extended:** Transferable once for 150 credit fee

### How to Transfer Warranty:

1. Seller provides buyer with:
   - Original purchase documentation
   - Product serial numbers
   - Maintenance records
   - Warranty registration confirmation

2. Buyer contacts ORSM within 30 days of purchase:
   - Provide proof of purchase/transfer
   - Pay transfer fee
   - Update registration information

3. ORSM updates warranty registration:
   - Warranty transferred to new owner
   - New owner receives confirmation
   - Remaining warranty period applies

### Used Equipment Purchased Without Warranty Transfer:

- **90-day limited warranty** provided by ORSM on used equipment
- Covers: Manufacturing defects only
- Does not cover: Wear, prior damage, improper maintenance by previous owner
- Can purchase extended warranty after inspection

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to keep my receipt?**
A: Yes, but if you register your product, ORSM has the purchase record. Keep receipt as backup.

**Q: Can I do my own repairs under warranty?**
A: Yes, as long as you use ORSM parts and follow ORSM procedures. Document your work. Labor isn't covered (you're doing it), but parts are free.

**Q: What if I can't get to a certified service center?**
A: ORSM will ship parts to you for DIY installation, or you can use a local mechanic (you pay labor). Remote support available.

**Q: Do firmware updates void warranty?**
A: No, official ORSM firmware updates do not void warranty. Unofficial/third-party firmware may void warranty.

**Q: What if my warranty claim is denied?**
A: You'll receive explanation. If you disagree, you can request review by warranty supervisor. You can also file appeal with documentation.

**Q: Can I buy warranty after the product fails?**
A: No, warranty must be purchased before failure occurs. Exception: Some extended warranties can be purchased before standard warranty expires.

**Q: Does warranty cover emergency support calls?**
A: Yes, warranty includes access to 24/7 technical support. You can call anytime for help, regardless of whether issue is warranty-covered.

**Q: What happens if ORSM discontinues my product?**
A: Warranty honored for full warranty period. Replacement parts available for minimum 10 years after discontinuation.

**Q: Are software updates free?**
A: Yes, software and firmware updates are always free, even after warranty expires.
